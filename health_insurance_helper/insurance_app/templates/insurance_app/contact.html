Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HealthGuard

What are the eligibility criteria for insurance?
You must be between 18-65 years of age and a citizen or permanent resident. You should be in general good health, though we accept applicants with pre-existing conditions.
How long does the claim approval process take?
Most claims are processed within 5-7 business days. Emergency claims are prioritized and may be processed within 24-48 hours. You can track your claim status in real-time through your dashboard.
Can I change my plan after enrollment?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan once per year during the annual enrollment period, or if you experience a qualifying life event such as marriage, birth, or job change.
Are family members covered under the Family Plan?
Yes, the Family Plan covers up to 4 family members including yourself. Covered members typically include spouse and dependent children up to age 25.
Is there a waiting period for claims?
There is typically a 30-day waiting period for non-emergency treatments from the date of enrollment. Emergency claims are covered immediately. The Premium plan offers a no waiting period option.
